FAQs

How do I submit my project?

You can submit your project either through our online form or via email.

Can I communicate with freelancers during the project

No, direct communication with freelancers is not facilitated. However, you will have a designated project incharge whom you can connect with for updates and inquiries regarding your project's progress.

How do you ensure quality?

We rigorously vet freelancers and provide tools for tracking progress and ensuring quality deliverables.

Whom do I need to Pay, and what is the Payment Process?

Once your project is finalized, a payment link will be shared with you. Payments are processed through this link, with a 50% advance payment required upfront. The remaining 50% is split into 25% upon reaching the project's halfway point, and the final 25% upon project completion.

Will I receive a refund if the Project exceeds the timeframe?

Yes, if your project is not completed within the agreed-upon timeframe, you will receive a full refund of the amount paid. Terms and conditions apply, so please refer to our refund policy for further details.

Is my Project Idea Secure when I submit it to you?

Yes, we take the security of your project idea seriously. Through our platform, both clients and freelancers are required to sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) to ensure the confidentiality of your project throughout the entire process. Your ideas are safe with us.
